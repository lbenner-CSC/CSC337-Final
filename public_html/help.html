How the Games Work

Fruit Bonanza

BlackJack

If you would rather try and play some Blackjack then you can click the blackjack button and be redirected to the blackjack page. There you will be able to play a game of blackjack. To understand Blackjack you need to know that aces count as 11's, any face card is a 10, and all other cards have their face value. You will get 2 cards and be shown how many points you have. You will also be able to see one of the dealers cards. You then have 2 options, you can either hit or stay. If you hit you get another card, if you stay its then the dealers turn. The idea is for you to get as close to 21 as possible without going over. To win you have to have a hand that is higher points than the dealers without going over 21.
