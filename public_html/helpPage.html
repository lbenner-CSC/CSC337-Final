How the Games Work

Fruit Bonanza

If a user like yourself wants to play some slots, then once you login you will be able to click the slot machine and be redirected to the slot machine page. There you can click anywhere on the slot machine to test your luck. You can win by getting at least 2 symbols in a row starting from the left reel.

BlackJack

If you would rather try and play some Blackjack then you can click the blackjack button and be redirected to the blackjack page. There you will be able to play a game of blackjack. Aces count as 11's, any face card is a 10, and all other cards have their face value. You will get 2 cards and shown how many points you have. You will also be able to see one of the dealers cards. You then have 2 options, you can either hit or stay. If you hit you get another card, if you stay its then the dealers turn.
